OFFICIAL USE NOTICE

This tool provides estimates only. Official PEBD determinations must be validated through proper Marine Corps channels per PAA 04-25. Do not input classified or sensitive personal information.

DODFMR Chapter 1 Rule: Time loss periods only affect Enlisted Marines. Officers' PEBDs are not changed by time loss periods.

Select Calculation Mode

If a PEBD discrepancy is identified by a MCTFS "PEBD Risk" advisory or through this calculator:

  1. Marine: Notify your servicing PAC or reporting unit via Electronic Personnel Admin Request (EPAR) to verify and address the PEBD discrepancy.
  2. PAC / Reporting Unit: Use the OMPF and the MCTFS D188 remark to analyze and validate periods of creditable service. If periods are missing, update the D188 remark and upload Key Supporting Documents to the OMPF.
  3. Escalation: Where instances of incorrect PEBD are confirmed, follow PRIUM instructions and forward cases to MMPB-21 at SMB_Pay_Entry_Base_Date_Correction@usmc.mil
  4. MMPB-21: Reviews case material and forwards PEBDs requiring adjustment to MISSO 9 for reporting.

It is the Marine's responsibility to ensure all service data is annotated in their records. Missing data, particularly regarding prior service, can cause calculation errors even when no PEBD advisory is issued.
