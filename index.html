    MARADMIN 052/26

    What's Next - Corrective Action Workflow

    If this PEBD calculation differs from the MCTFS record or your MARADMIN 052/26 advisory, follow these steps in order:

    1

    Marine

    Notify your servicing PAC or reporting unit via Electronic Personnel Admin Request (EPAR) to verify and address the PEBD discrepancy.

    2

    PAC / Reporting Unit

    Use the OMPF and the MCTFS D188 remark to validate periods of creditable service. Update the D188 remark and upload Key Supporting Documents to the OMPF if periods are missing.

    3

    MMPB-21 Escalation

    For confirmed PEBD errors, follow PRIUM instructions and forward cases to MMPB-21 at the email below.

    4

    MISSO 9

    MMPB-21 reviews case material and forwards PEBDs requiring adjustment to MISSO 9 for reporting.

    Marines are responsible for ensuring all service data is annotated in their records. Missing prior service data causes calculation errors even without a PEBD advisory.
