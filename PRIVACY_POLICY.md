# Privacy Policy
Marine Corps PEBD Calculator by Semper Admin

**Effective Date:** June 2, 2026
**Last Updated:** June 2, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

The Marine Corps PEBD Calculator ("Tool") is a client-side, browser-based calculation utility designed to assist with understanding creditable service calculations under PAA 04-25. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

---

## 2. Data We Do NOT Collect

**Zero Data Collection.**

The PEBD Calculator:
- Does NOT transmit any user input to external servers
- Does NOT use cookies to track you
- Does NOT use analytics or telemetry (Google Analytics, Segment, etc.)
- Does NOT store your calculations in a database
- Does NOT log your IP address or device information
- Does NOT require user accounts or authentication
- Does NOT share data with third parties

All calculations run entirely within your web browser. Your military service dates, PEBD values, and period details are processed locally and never leave your device unless you manually export or print them.

---

## 3. Data Processing

### Calculation Data
When you enter service period information and click "Calculate PEBD," the following occurs:
1. Your input is stored temporarily in your browser's RAM (memory)
2. JavaScript performs date calculations using standard JavaScript date functions
3. Results are displayed on screen
4. Data is cleared from memory when you:
   - Refresh the page
   - Close the browser tab
   - Clear browser cache
   - Click the "Reset All" button

### Print/Export
If you print or export your calculation report:
- PDF/print files are created locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to cloud services
- Your browser's print dialog handles the output
